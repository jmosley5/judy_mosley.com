"Lots of People Wear Glasses" is the story of Katie, a big four, discovering that before she enters Kindergarten, she'll need glasses. But, who does she know that wears glasses?

At it's core, "Lots of People Wear Glasses" is the story of transition and the discovery that all of us, in our differences, have so much in common. This is the story of embracing who we are and how we all benefit from being ourselves.


Judy Mosley is an author, blogger, budding web developer, and crochet fanatic. A former contributer to Make The Days Count and the writer for Waking Up, she loves scribbling down whatever grabs her attention. She lives in Kentucky with her blacksmith husband and her three attention-grabbing children. Lots of People Wear Glasses is her first book.
