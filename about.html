About

It all began with watching her mother put thoughts down to pen and paper. At age 11, Judy started her own journal and discovered the joy of writing. Since then, her works have appeared inMake The Days Count and Waking Up, her online journey of life, love, and living the moment. "Lots of People Wear Glasses" is her first book.
